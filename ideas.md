Game ideas:
- Have a game with lines that are all colors. When you cross a color it changes the color of all intersecting lines. There is a finish line that has a specific color that you need to cross it as in order to win. There are lasers (the lines) that are spread accross the board, and two/three starting points that are the color white (the lasers have orbs on each end with the YRB pip). You need angle your two starting lasers so that they pass through several colored lasers and mix to equal the goal line's color. Both goals must be crossed with the correct laser color to win. If your lasers ever intersect with eachother, they will change their color accordingly. If you have a red laser, and a blue laser, they will equal a purple laser. We may have negative lasers that remove a certain color. Such as negative yellow. If you cross the laser without yellow, it does nothing. If you cross the negative yellow with green it will become blue. If you cross it with brown, it will become purple.
- Some sort of game where you have a cluster of numbers in orbs that are relationally sized to the number (1 is smallest, 5 is five times bigger). You only have so many "bombs" of various different numbers. They deal appropriate damage, so if you use a 1 bomb it will do -1 damage and detory the 1 orbs. It explodes in a small radius, so if you use 2 1 damage bombs on a 5, it will do a total of 2 damage and although the orb will keep its size, it will have 3. If you destroy bigger orbs, they have a bigger blast radius. So you have limited attacks and want to get the biggest score by destroying certain orbs first.
